AgroSense Node 2.0

  • Build Specifications:
  • ESP32-S3-MINI-1U-H4, E22-900MM22S LoRa Module, DS3231 Real-Time Clock, LT3652 MPPT Solar Charger (5–32 V), TP5000 Li-ion Charger, DW06D Reverse-Current Protection, SY6280 Power Switch, RT8059 Buck Converter (3.3 V), MP3429GL Boost Converters (5 V & 9/12 V), MAX485 RS-485 Transceiver, CP2102N USB-UART Bridge, Custom 24-layer stackup PCB (82 × 80 mm) with 4 copper layers, 195 Components, Double-Sided Assembly
    Project Description

    AgroSense 2.0 is a precision-agriculture sensor node built on a custom 4-Copper layer PCB to deliver robust, long-range monitoring with optimized power management. It integrates an ESP32-S3 microcontroller and E22-900MM22S LoRa radio, supported by a high-efficiency LT3652 MPPT charger for solar harvesting and TP5000/DW06D/SY6280 circuitry for smart battery charging and protection.

    Known Design Challenges

    During initial testing, a routing oversight on the CP2102 USB traces resulted in Reverse connectivity— D+ and D- lines, causing USB connectivity Failure. Additionally, the MT3608L boost converter failed to trace properly due to insufficient knowledge about the poly trace and groud connectivity vias. Both issues have been documented for the 2.0 revision.

    To overcome these challenges, AgroSense 2.0 integrates MPPT-based solar charging (LT3652) with TP5000/DW06D battery protection, ensuring reliable charge management and extended field uptime.critical footprints were revised to prevent routing and thermal issues.

    The compact double-sided design (82 × 80 mm) accommodates 195 components, balancing component density with signal integrity, thermal performance. AgroSense 2.0 demonstrates a scalable, energy-efficient platform for modern agricultural IoT deployments.
