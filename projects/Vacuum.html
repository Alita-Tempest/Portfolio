Vacuum Cleaning Robot

  • Build Specifications:
  • Arduino Uno, HC-05 Bluetooth Module, Dual H-Bridge Motor Driver (L298N), DC Motors & Wheels, Ultrasonic Sensors, IR Line Sensors, Centrifugal fan, Li-ion Battery Power Supply, Custom Chassis
    Project Description

    Built an autonomous vacuum-cleaning rover on a custom acrylic chassis, featuring Arduino Uno control, HC-05 Bluetooth start/stop, and a Li-ion battery power supply. The frame houses dual DC motors driven by an L298N H-bridge for smooth locomotion, while an onboard centrifugal fan and PWM-controlled sweep brush generate suction and debris collection.

    Collision and cliff avoidance are handled by HC-SR04 ultrasonic sensors plus an IR edge detector.

    Real-time status is sent over Serial for debugging, and a toggle switch arms or disarms cleaning, making it easy to extend with additional modes or user interfaces.
